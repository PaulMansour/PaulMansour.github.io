About

This is the personal APL blog of Paul Mansour.

The subtitle of this blog is taken from a series of math books written by J.E. Thompson: Arithmetic for the Practical Man, Algebra for the Practical Man, Geometry for the Practical Man etc. I believe that Richard Feynman tought himself math from these books. This is, I think, the only thing we have in common.
